Account Deletion Request

FinFlow Application

FinFlow provides users with the ability to permanently delete their account and all associated data. This page explains how you can request full account deletion.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

Once your account deletion request is processed, the following will be permanently removed:

  • Your FinFlow account and login credentials
  • All financial records, including transactions, budgets, and categories
  • Any synchronized or backed-up data associated with your account

How to Request Account Deletion

To request permanent deletion of your FinFlow account, please send an email with the details below:

Email Subject: Account Deletion Request – FinFlow

Please include the email address used to register your FinFlow account and clearly state that you are requesting permanent account deletion.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Some minimal technical or legal data may be retained if required by law
  • Deletion requests are processed after identity verification
